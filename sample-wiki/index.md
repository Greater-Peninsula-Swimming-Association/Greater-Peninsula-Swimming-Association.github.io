Welcome to the **Greater Peninsula Swimming Association (GPSA) Wiki** - your comprehensive resource for league information, rules, procedures, and historical records.

## What is GPSA?

The Greater Peninsula Swimming Association is a youth summer swim league serving teams across the Virginia Peninsula. Our mission is to provide a fun, competitive, and educational swimming environment for swimmers of all skill levels.
